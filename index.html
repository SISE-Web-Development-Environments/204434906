About Me


My name is Rina Treidel, I was born in Berlin in 1922.
I had a brother who was two years older than me.
I was 11 years old when Hitler came to power, and from that moment our life changed forever.
On Crystal Night, November 1938, I went with my friend from school and it was very dangerous.
We passed by our synagogue and it was burning.
My parents managed to arrange a large sum of money in order to save me,
and sent me to Palestine on March 1939 to a boarding school named Ayanot.
I stayed there for 2 years.

I met my future husband who served in the British army during the second world war.
We got married after the war in 1946.

I gave birth to 2 daughters.

With my husband and the daughters, we enjoyed hiking every Saturday.
We had a large circle of friends with whom we used to travel abroad.

For the last 20 years I am living in an old age home in Haifa.
Today, I am 97 years old, and I have 6 grandchildren and 8 great grandchildren.
